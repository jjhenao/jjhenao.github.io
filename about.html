About Me

Hello,

  • I have a PhD in Environmental Engineering from the University of Antioquia, Medellin, Colombia.
  • I am currently a postdoc in the Division of Atmospheric Sciences at DRI (dri.edu), working on urban climate modeling and air quality modeling.
  • My research is focused on the interactions between meteorology, pollutant emissions, air quality, and urbanization, relying on the implementation of high-resolution numerical models and analyzing observations from in-situ and remote sensors.
